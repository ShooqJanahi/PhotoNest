Frequently Asked Questions

Welcome to our FAQ section where we answer your most common queries about PhotoNest. Here, you’ll find detailed responses on everything.

  • What is PhotoNest?

    PhotoNest is a platform designed to help you effortlessly organize, store, and share your photos with friends, family, or colleagues. With advanced tagging and search features, it ensures your memories are always accessible and well-organized.

  • Is PhotoNest free to use?

    Yes, PhotoNest is completely free to use, offering all of its features without any hidden charges or subscriptions.

  • How secure are my photos on PhotoNest?

    Your privacy and security are our top priorities. PhotoNest uses industry-standard encryption to protect your data, and we offer advanced privacy settings, including two-factor authentication, to keep your photos safe.

  • Can I share my albums with others?

    Yes, you can easily share albums with specific people by granting them access. You can control whether they can view, comment, or collaborate on the album.

  • What types of files can I upload?

    PhotoNest supports a wide range of file types, including common image formats such as JPEG, PNG, and GIF. Additionally, you can upload videos and other media formats depending on your project needs.
